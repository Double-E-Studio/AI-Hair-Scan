Everything you need to know about Hair Scan AI

Hair Scan AI is a mobile application that uses artificial intelligence (Google Gemini AI) to analyze your scalp health. By capturing 4 photos of your scalp, the app generates a detailed report covering hair density, Norwood scale evaluation, volume, and overall scalp condition.
The app is free to download. Scans can be unlocked by watching rewarded ads (3 short ads) or by spending coins earned within the app. There is no mandatory subscription.
Hair Scan AI is available for both iOS and Android devices. You can download it from the App Store or Google Play.
Hair Scan AI is developed by DoubleE Studio, created by Michele Cipriani and Tommaso Vilotto. For questions or support, contact us at doublee.studio.team@gmail.com.
Yes. Hair Scan AI is intended for users who are at least 18 years old. Users under 18 may not use the application.
After unlocking a scan, you capture 4 photos of your scalp: front, right side, left side, and top. The app sends these images to Google Gemini AI, which analyzes them and returns a detailed text report. Photos are never saved on our servers — they are deleted from memory after analysis.
Each scan report includes:
  • Hair Density — Percentage score (0-100%) indicating scalp coverage
  • Norwood Scale — Stage 1 through 7 hair loss classification
  • Volume — Percentage score (0-100%) measuring hair fullness
  • Overall Condition — Percentage score (0-100%) combining quality and coverage
  • Overall Review — AI-generated narrative with observations and suggestions
There are two ways to unlock a scan:
  • From onboarding path: watch 3 short rewarded ads via Google AdMob.
  • From the home area: spend 3 coins from your in-app coin balance.
Coins can be earned through the app's reward system.
The onboarding asks optional questions about your age, sex, and any existing hair or health concerns. This information is used only to personalize your initial experience — it is not uploaded to any server and is stored locally on your device only.
